Extension 1

For the first extension, we just took the let statement and the fully simplified equation (⅛x-3½=2) and replaced the 2 with a four and solved from there. The steps were:
Isolate the variable by using the property of equality

⅛x=7½

Continue isolating the variable

x=7½÷⅛

Multiply with the reciprocal

x=15/2•8/1

Cross simplify

x=15•4

Multiply

x=60

Remove variable and add unit

60 pennies
