Welcome to Piper's 7th Grade Penny Wars POW!

In this POW we are asked to figure out how many pennies Mrs. Robinson has after giving half of her pennies and 2 more, then half of what she has left and 2 more, then half of what she has left and 2 more, and she ends with 2 pennies. To solve this POW we had to use my skills of writing variable equations, solving variable equations, the distributive property, multiplying and dividing fractions, combining like terms, critical thinking, and group work/cooperation.
