1¼ cups all-purpose flour
¾ teaspoon baking soda
½ teaspoon baking powder
¼ cup unsalted butter, softened
1 cup peanut butter, room temperature
¾ cup sugar
½ cup firmly packed brown sugar
1 large egg, room temperature
1 tablespoon milk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup chocolate chips
1+ tablespoons powdered, for sprinkling
Preheat oven to 350° Fahrenheit.
In a large bowl, mix the flour, baking soda, baking powder, and the salt.
Set aside.
In another large bowl, beat the butter and peanut butter until fluffy.
Add the sugars (don’t add the powdered though) and beat until smooth.
Add the egg and mix well. Add the milk and the vanilla extract.
Then add the contents of the other large bowl. Stir in the chocolate chips.
Place the sprinkling sugar on a plate.
Shape the dough into about teaspoon size spheres.
Gently roll the spheres on the sugar plate. Then place the spheres on a greased cookie sheet, a few inches apart. Bake in oven for 10-12 minutes.
Enjoy!
